I love tweet in the Morning


People are known to have different moods during different times of the day. In order to find out whether people are more sad in morning or night, the city with most sad tweets vs the part of the day analysis is performed. The time of the day was divided into four parts: 6.00 am to 12 noon, 12 noon to 6 pm, 6 pm to 12.00 am, and 12.00 am to 6.00 am.
